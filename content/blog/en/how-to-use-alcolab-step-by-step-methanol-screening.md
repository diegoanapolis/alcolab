## How to Use AlcoLab: Practical Guide for Screening

Methanol detection in beverages is now within reach of everyone. With **AlcoLab**, anyone does complete screening at home using simple materials.

This guide shows exactly how to use the application to ensure beverage safety. The process divides into **four main stages**:

1. Density measurement
2. Temperature measurement
3. Viscosity measurement
4. Result analysis

It takes only **15-25 minutes** from start to finish. Therefore, how to use AlcoLab is accessible to anyone, anywhere.

## What You Need: Essential Materials

Before starting, gather all necessary materials. The list is simple and total cost stays below **R$10**.

### Mandatory Materials
- 20mL syringe with 22G needle (sterile)
- Kitchen scale with 0.1g resolution
- Smartphone with internet access
- Distilled water (for calibration)
- Sample of beverage to test

### Optional but Recommended Materials
- Thermometer with 20°C-30°C scale
- Paper towel or fabric for cleaning

This way, you'll have everything needed for accurate screening. Therefore, how to use AlcoLab requires minimal investment.

## Compatible Beverages: What Can Be Tested

Not all alcoholic beverages can be tested. AlcoLab works with pure distilled spirits of high alcohol content. In this sense, there are important limitations.

### Compatible Beverages
- Vodka
- Cachaça
- Whisky
- Rum
- Gin
- Tequila
- Pisco
- Tiquira

### Incompatible Beverages
- Liqueurs
- Beers
- Wines
- Mixed drinks or cocktails
- Flavored distillates
- Beverages turbid or with sediment

This limitation exists because density and viscosity of beverages with sugar, gases, or other components are significantly different. Therefore, this specificity ensures greater precision exactly in highest-risk beverages. After all, pure distilled beverages are most frequently adulterated.

## Step 1: Density Measurement

Density measurement compares weight of specific volume of water with weight of same volume of beverage. It's the first essential stage.

**Detailed Instructions:**

1. Place scale on flat, stable surface
2. Calibrate scale per manufacturer instructions
3. Weigh empty syringe + needle (note as m₀)
4. Fill syringe with 20mL distilled water
5. Weigh again (note as m_water)
6. Clean syringe with paper towel
7. Fill with 20mL of sample and weigh (m_sample)
8. Calculate: ρ_rel = m_sample / m_water

This way, you'll have the first essential measurement. Furthermore, it's important to use the same syringe and needle for all measurements on same day. Therefore, consistency is critical for accuracy.

## Step 2: Temperature Measurement (Optional But Recommended)

Temperature slightly affects physical properties of beverages. Although optional, measuring temperature significantly increases result accuracy.

**How to Measure:**

1. Let sample rest for a few minutes
2. Submerge thermometer in beverage inside syringe
3. Wait to stabilize (usually 30-60 seconds)
4. Note temperature (preferably 20-30°C)

Furthermore, also record temperature of distilled water used in calibration. In short, these two measurements let the app make precise corrections. Therefore, temperature is an important detail for how to use AlcoLab correctly.

## Step 3: Viscosity Measurement

Viscosity measurement is the **most important stage**. Detects difference between ethanol and methanol through the liquid flow speed.

### Syringe Preparation
1. Completely remove plunger from syringe
2. Fix syringe vertically (needle pointing down)
3. Fill with liquid above the 18mL mark
4. Let drain naturally

### Filming Procedure: Water
1. Prepare smartphone in video mode
2. Point at syringe, film markings clearly
3. When liquid column reaches 18mL, start recording
4. Continue filming until 14mL mark
5. Record **at least 2 repetitions** of same procedure

### Filming Procedure: Sample
1. Carefully clean syringe (DON'T remove needle)
2. Fill with sample above 18mL
3. Repeat exactly the same procedure as water
4. Record **at least 2 videos** of the sample

This way, app calculates relative viscosity with precision. Furthermore, it's fundamental that procedure be **identical** for water and sample. Therefore, how to use AlcoLab requires technical precision in this stage.

## Step 4: Analysis in AlcoLab Application

After collecting all measurements, it's time to use the app to interpret results. This is when how to use AlcoLab becomes evident.

### Accessing the Application
1. Open browser and access **https://alcolab.org**
2. Click "Start screening" button
3. Fill in initial information:
   - Beverage type (vodka, cachaça, whisky, etc.)
   - Declared alcohol content (ABV% from label)
   - Date of analysis

### Entering Density Data
1. Enter m₀ value (empty syringe + needle)
2. Enter m_water (syringe + needle + 20mL water)
3. Enter m_sample (syringe + needle + 20mL beverage)
4. Fill temperature if measured

### Submitting Viscosity Videos
1. Click "Upload water video"
2. Select first video file
3. Use marking tool to indicate when meniscus crosses marks 18, 17, 16, 15, and 14mL
4. App processes automatically
5. Repeat for second water video
6. Do same procedure for sample videos

In short, all analysis happens locally. Furthermore, no data is sent to remote servers. Therefore, your privacy is guaranteed. After all, local processing is fundamental.

## Interpreting Results: Safety Traffic Light

After processing all data, AlcoLab displays result in simple and intuitive visual.

### Green Result
The beverage is compatible with pure ethanol. No signs of significant methanol presence. You can consume with greater relative safety.

**Important:** However, if there are other adulteration signs (very low price, bad label, suspicious origin), we don't recommend consumption even with green result.

### Yellow Result
Collected data aren't conclusive. App recommends repeating screening with more attention to measurement precision.

### Red Result
There's strong indication of possible methanol presence. It's recommended not to consume beverage and seek supplementary analysis at official lab.

This way, interpretation is immediate. Requires no prior technical knowledge. Therefore, how to use AlcoLab makes safety accessible.

## Important Tips for Better Accuracy

For best results, consider these practical recommendations.

### Measurement Quality
- Use same syringe and needle for water and sample
- Complete all measurements same day
- Don't remove needle between density measurements
- Keep scale on flat, stable surface

### Filming Technique
- Use good lighting (natural light or LED)
- Keep camera fixed and focused on markings
- Film meniscus (not air bubble)
- Ensure 18-14mL marks are clearly visible

### Initial Validation
- Recommended: test control with pharmacy ethanol (70% INPM)
- This validates your technique before testing unknown samples
- Furthermore, significantly increases confidence in results

Therefore, how to use AlcoLab with precision requires attention to these details. After all, measurement quality determines result reliability.

## Technology Behind: How App Processes Data

AlcoLab's true power lies in innovative technology. App uses **Python/Pyodide** — complete Python implementation running in WebAssembly directly in browser.

This means all mathematical and statistical analysis happens locally. In short, data never leaves your device. Security and privacy are totally guaranteed.

Furthermore, this approach lets app work offline. Once loaded first time, you use AlcoLab without internet. Therefore, how to use AlcoLab is secure because processing is 100% local.
